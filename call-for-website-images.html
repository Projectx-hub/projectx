Call for Website Images

We need images for the site. These can be photos or visual art. This is a nice opportunity to promote your work at the same time.

For websites (in general), we prefer the following.

  1. For photography, please submit original aspect ratios (e.g. 2:3) or precropped 1:1 if square formats are your style.
  2. A medium-high resolution copy of the final image. E.g. longest side ranging 2,200-4,016 pixels (file sizes 2-5 MB).

Unfortunately, we can't accept images from Instagram. IG compresses images to 1,080 px. This doesn't give us enough to work with. We also want to avoid extra rounds of compression/saving to preserve original quality.

Thanks for your support!
